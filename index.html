About Us

Football Zone is a passion project built for football fans who live and breathe the beautiful game.

Our Story

Football Zone was born from a simple idea — football is more than just a sport. It's culture, identity, passion, and community. We wanted to build a space where fans could explore their football personality, test their knowledge, and read honest takes on the game they love.

We launched in 2025 with two features: the Football Personality Engine and the Career Simulator. Since then, we've grown into a full platform with quizzes, blogs, and more coming every week.

What We Do

Football Personality Engine — Answer 4 simple questions and find out which elite footballer you are most like. Based on your club, position, style, and personality.

Career Simulator — Live out your dream football career through a series of choices. Will you become a World Class Legend, or a One Club hero?

Quizzes — Test your football knowledge with our growing library of quizzes — from easy Gen-Z checks to brutal Champions League trivia.

Blog — Real opinions and analysis on the game. No clickbait, no fake news — just honest football writing.

Our Mission

To make football more fun, more interactive, and more personal for every fan — whether you just started watching or you've been following the game for decades.
